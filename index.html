About RHJ Sweets

For 100 years, Ram Lal Hakumat Rai (RHJ) has stood for purity, craftsmanship, and family tradition. Every sweet is made with care and freshly packed to deliver the true RHJ taste that has been trusted by generations.

Our commitment to quality has made us a trusted name in Indian sweets. We use only the finest ingredients - pure desi ghee, fresh dry fruits, and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our online store brings our timeless recipes and trusted quality straight to your door. We believe in maintaining the authenticity and richness that has made RHJ the choice of sweet lovers across India.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What makes RHJ sweets different from others? ⌄
RHJ sweets are made with 100% pure desi ghee, finest ingredients, and traditional recipes passed down through 100 years. Every sweet is freshly prepared and packed to ensure maximum freshness and authenticity that you won't find elsewhere.
❓ What is the shelf life of your products? ⌄
Most of our sweets have a shelf life of 7-10 days when stored properly in cool conditions. Dry sweets like biscuits last longer (up to 30 days). Each product comes with detailed storage instructions on the packaging.
